[**The Human Inside Project**](https://sites.google.com/view/humaninside/home) Part 1: I'm not a robot, is a citizen science project that will rely on crowdsourced data. Please do participate, it takes between 15 and 30 minutes to complete, and it'd be lovely to see your photo on the [Humans Inside page](https://sites.google.com/view/humaninside/humans-inside)<br> To participate, you can use the form below for English, or go via the [project site](https://sites.google.com/view/humaninside/home) if you would like French. Mandarin will be available mid-May 2024. First you'll answer some basic questions about yourself, then you'll get a page with a text written by AI. Your task is to edit that text in whatever way you deem necessary (modification, addition, deletion of words, letters, it's really up to you...) so that in your eyes, the new version reads less like one written by a bot, and more like one written by a human.<br>

If you are a linguist with a specialization other than English, and you would like collaborate as language lead, please do get in touch with me (mail icon in footer).
