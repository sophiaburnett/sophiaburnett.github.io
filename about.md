"White Flag Typography", my Ph.D thesis supervised by Julien Longhi, was successfully defended at CY Cergy Paris University on December 1st, 2023. The thesis concerns discourse analysis in Computer Mediated Communications, and proposes a taxonomy of 6 usages of non-standard minuscules, in particular the non-standard 1SG. It draws on a wide interdisciplinary and theoretical base, applying notions such as indexicality, enregisterment, and iconicity, and combining them with elements of cognitive theory and social psychology to provide a multifacetted yet deeply analysed (experimental) ‘profile’ for each of the six usages. This research included the study of the phenomenon's potential embodied and physiological isomorphisms of depression and of [anorexia nervosa](https://doi.org/10.31219/osf.io/gz72t) <br>
